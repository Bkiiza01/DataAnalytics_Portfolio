Exploratory Data Analysis
& Predictive Modeling

@DashApp hosted on PythonAnywhere

Description:

In this project, I performed exploratory data analysis on a dataset of second-hand car sales on eBay. The goal was to gain insights into the market dynamics, understand key trends, and identify factors affecting pricing and sales patterns.

Key Accomplishments:

  • Explored and visualized the distribution of car brands, models, and prices, providing a comprehensive overview of the market.
  • Identified outliers and anomalies in the dataset, helping to identify potential data errors or interesting cases for further investigation.
  • Uncovered correlations between factors such as mileage, age, and price, providing valuable insights into pricing dynamics and customer preferences.
  • Conducted in-depth analysis of the impact of variables like vehicle type, fuel type, and damage status on sales performance, enabling strategic decision-making.

Methodologies Used:

  • Data cleaning and preprocessing to ensure data quality and integrity.
  • Utilized statistical techniques to analyze distributions, detect outliers, and identify patterns.
  • Employed data visualization techniques including histograms, scatter plots, and box plots to present key insights effectively.
  • Leveraged statistical modeling to explore correlations and relationships between variables.

Impact of Your Work:

The EDA provided a comprehensive understanding of the second-hand car sales market on eBay. The insights gained helped stakeholders make data-driven decisions, such as optimizing pricing strategies, identifying popular car models, and understanding customer preferences. The analysis also contributed to a deeper understanding of the factors influencing sales performance, facilitating future predictive analysis and modeling efforts.
