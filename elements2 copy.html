US traffic Volume
Dashboard

In this project, I developed a comprehensive dashboard to track traffic volume in Minnesota at a yearly, monthly, daily, and hourly level. By analyzing this data, the dashboard provides valuable insights for state road maintenance and construction projects. It facilitates informed decision-making by identifying traffic patterns, congestion hotspots, and peak hours, enabling efficient allocation of resources and improved planning for infrastructure projects.

AirBnB Performance in
Athens

In this project, I developed a dynamic dashboard showcasing the average price of AirBnB listings in Athens by different cities. Leveraging tools like Tableau, I curated an interactive visualization that allows users to explore and compare pricing trends across various city areas, aiding travelers and hosts in making informed decisions about AirBnB accommodations in Athens.

Sales
Dashboard

In this project, I developed an Excel dashboard to showcase sales data in a concise and visually appealing manner. The dashboard provides actionable insights for informed decision-making and enhances the understanding of sales performance.
